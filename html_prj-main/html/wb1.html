Welcome to Horizon

"Chasing Speed, Living the Dream"

Horizon Racing is one of the most innovative and passionate Formula-style racing teams. We focus on speed, technology, and teamwork to dominate tracks worldwide. Every lap is a story of precision, dedication, and the relentless pursuit of victory.

Founded with the vision to push boundaries, Horizon has become a symbol of resilience and innovation. Our drivers, engineers, and pit crew share one dream — to stand at the top of the podium and inspire the next generation of racers.
